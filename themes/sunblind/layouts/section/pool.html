COVID-19

In All King County Phases

  • A COVID waiver must be signed or updated for every member and household.
  • Masks must be worn in lobby and restrooms.
  • Social distancing must be followed with people outside your household.

In Phase 3 (Current Phase)

No reservations in this phase; we will have open swim with up to 28 people in the pool.

Social distancing is required for non-household members.

Showers and changing rooms will be open, but social distancing must be followed.

Guests are allowed with HOA/Pool pass holder, up to 4 guests, $5 each.

Limit of 2 hours at the pool will be enforced if other members are waiting to use the pool. 2 Hour Protocol: The first lot to check in that has hit 2 hours, will be asked to leave if members are waiting to enter the pool.

Pool Parties and Rentals

Parties and rentals are available with a maximum of 25 people in Phase 3.

HOA/Pool pass holder hosts must abide by all COVID procedures, follow social distancing protocols and be present at the party.

NO outside food or drink.

NO showers or changing areas open. Restrooms and sinks will be available.

All patrons on deck and in the pool must have a COVID waiver signed.

Prices: $100 for one hour, $200 for 2 hours.

Reservations: Begin May 29th, with a 2-week advance notice required, subject to manager approval.

Reservations made at pool by HOA/Pool pass holder only.

Lifeguards

We have 2 certified lifeguards on duty at all times. No one under the age of 7 will be allowed in the deep end without an adult. A swim test will be given to all other young swimmers before being allowed in the deep end.

Children who are 9 and older and have passed the swim test may come to the pool by themselves. Artificial swimming aids are only allowed in the pool when accompanied by an adult. All of our lifeguards are authorized to eject anyone from the pool grounds for any reason at anytime.
