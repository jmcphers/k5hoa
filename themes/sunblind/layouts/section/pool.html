Pool Parties and Rentals

Reservations made at pool by HOA/Pool pass holder only.

Parties and rentals are available for HOA or Pool Pass members, and they must be present at the party.

Parties over 25 people must notify the manager 1 week in advance, due to needing a 3rd guard. Also, an additional $75 dollars for the party will be charged.

You can have outside food or drink, no alcohol.

Floaties and toys are allowed for parties only.

Prices: $110 for one hour, $220 for 2 hours.

Reservations: Begin May 27th, with a 2-week advance notice required, subject to manager approval.

We allow grandchildren for free, with HOA members; they count toward the 4 people allowed per visit.

Lifeguards

We have 2 certified lifeguards on duty at all times. No one under the age of 7 will be allowed in the deep end without an adult. A swim test will be given to all other young swimmers before being allowed in the deep end.

Children who are 9 and older and have passed the swim test may come to the pool by themselves. Artificial swimming aids are only allowed in the pool when accompanied by an adult. All of our lifeguards are authorized to eject anyone from the pool grounds for any reason at anytime.
